Mango



The mango, India's national fruit, is a popular and versatile fruit enjoyed in various forms.

It can be consumed raw, in salads, or used to make juices, jams, milkshakes, and pickles.

Mangoes are also known for their nutritional value, being a rich source of vitamins and minerals.

A popular mango variety in India is the Alphonso mango, often associated with summer and known for its delicious taste and exclusive quality.
